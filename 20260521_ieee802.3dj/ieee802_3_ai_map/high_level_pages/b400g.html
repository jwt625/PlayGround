IEEE 802.3 Beyond 400 Gb/s Ethernet Study Group

With the approval of the IEEE P802.3df PAR on 8 December 2021, the IEEE 802.3 Beyond 400 Gb/s Ethernet Study Group has completed its charter and transitioned to the IEEE P802.3df 200 Gb/s, 400 Gb/s, 800 Gb/s, and 1.6 Tb/s Ethernet Task Force.
The IEEE P802.3df Task Force home page is http://www.ieee802.org/3/df/.

The IEEE 802 LMSC Executive Committee has chartered a Study Group under the IEEE 802.3 Ethernet Working Group to develop a Project Authorization Request (PAR) and Criteria for Standards Development (CSD) responses for:

(1) Beyond 400 Gb/s Ethernet
(2) Physical Layer specifications for existing Ethernet rates based on Physical Layer specifications for beyond 400 Gb/s Ethernet.
