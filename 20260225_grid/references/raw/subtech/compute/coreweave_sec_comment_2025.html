Please file the Master Services Agreement with NVIDIA and the MagAI Capacity Agreement with MagAI Ventures as exhibits to your registration statement. Refer to Item 601(b)(10)(ii)(A) of Regulation S-K.

The Company respectfully acknowledges the Staff’s comment and advises the Staff that the Company does not believe it is required to file the Master Services Agreement with NVIDIA (the “NVIDIA MSA”) or the MagAI Capacity Agreement with MagAI Ventures (the “MagAI Capacity Agreement” and together the “Customer Agreements”) under Item 601(b)(10)(ii)(A) of Regulation S-K since the Customer Agreements are contracts that ordinarily accompany the kind of business conducted by the Company and are immaterial in amount or significance to the Company.

More specifically, Item 601(b)(10)(ii) of Regulation S-K states that “if the contract is such as ordinarily accompanies the kind of business conducted by the registrant and its subsidiaries, it will be deemed to have been made in the ordinary course of business and need not be filed unless it falls within one or more specified categories, in which case it shall be filed except where immaterial in amount or significance.”

The NVIDIA MSA is a standard committed customer contract solely for the purchase of the Company’s platform, solutions and services by NVIDIA, on the standard terms and conditions that are substantially similar to those terms and conditions of agreements with the Company’s other customers.
