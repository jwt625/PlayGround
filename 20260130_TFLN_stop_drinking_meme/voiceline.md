# Project Context: TFLN Technical Meme
# Original Source: Thai Health Promotion Foundation - "Just Stop Drinking" PSA
# Theme: Transitioning from the technical limitations of Thin Film Lithium Niobate (TFLN) to high-performance solutions.

## Video Structure
- 00:00 - 00:04: THE STRUGGLE. Fast cuts representing the inherent physical instabilities of TFLN.
- 00:05: THE CATALYST. The realization/application of Magnesium Oxide (MgO) doping.
- 00:06 - 00:28: THE ASCENSION. A rapid-fire success montage showing technical optimization leading to industrial revolution.
- 00:29: THE CALL TO ACTION. Final authoritative statement.

## Key Technical Definitions for Visual Alignment
- Pyroelectric Effect: Thermal instability causing charge buildup.
- GRIIRA: Green-Induced Infrared Absorption (power-dependent loss).
- EO Bias Drift: Movement of the operating point over time due to carrier migration.
- MgO Doping: The standard industry method to increase the photorefractive threshold.
- Blue LED Excitation: A method to "prime" carriers to mitigate drift.
- Vpi (Half-wave voltage): The voltage required for a phase shift of $\pi$. Lower is better.
